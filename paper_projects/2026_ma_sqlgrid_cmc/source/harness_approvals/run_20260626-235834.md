- Very recent related work is downgraded to contemporary preprint context, not treated as settled archival baselines or claim-defining comparisons.

The claim-evidence boundary is acceptable:

- Main accuracy claims match Stage 14: C2 `0.4389`, C4 `126/180` and `0.7000`, C5 `131/180` and `0.7278`, C4 prompt tokens `259.2` versus C2 `710.3`, and C5 latency `5562.5 ms`.
